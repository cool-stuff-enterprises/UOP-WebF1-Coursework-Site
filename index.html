About

This site is the product of my studies in the Web Foundation module on the Computer Science Degree at the University of Portsmouth. It will act as a worklog for my studies during my first year at University.

Building websites is something I have enjoyed since I was little. The first site I built was to host a stop-frame animation myself and a friend had created for an English assignment. I have come a long way since then, both in the quality of the sites I produce and my English skills.
